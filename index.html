Somali cuisine includes a variety of dishes like sabaayad (flatbread), canjeero (pancake-like bread), xalwo (halwa), sambuusa (samosas), and bariis iskukaris (spiced rice). Other popular foods include cambuulo (beans), muufo (Somali bread), and various meat dishes like hilib ari (roasted goat).
